Our Journey

in 4 year

Four years at Thammasat, we started from scratch, learning, problem-solving, and pushing each other forward. Together, we faced ups and downs, growing through every moment, and now we’re ready to take on the real world.

Year 1


- Starting and Adapting


    Our journey begins with learning the fundamentals of computer science together, such as programming, mathematics, computer systems, and algorithms. We all adapt to university life while building connections and friendships in the group, sharing experiences and helping each other out along the way.

Year 2


- Diving Deeper and Growing Together


    As a group, we dive deeper into complex subjects like data structures, databases, and operating systems. We start working on group projects, developing our teamwork skills, tackling challenges, and learning from each other's mistakes to grow stronger together.

Year 3


- Applying Knowledge and Tackling Challenges


    Our group applies the knowledge we've gained to real-world projects and internships. We collaborate on coding, solve practical problems, and learn how to work effectively as a team, gaining hands-on experience in software development and problem-solving.

Year 4


- Preparing for the Future


    In the final year, we focus on specialized subjects like app development, computer security, and complete our final projects. We prepare ourselves for the workforce, ready to face the challenges of the real world, but knowing we’ll continue to support each other as a team, even beyond graduation.
